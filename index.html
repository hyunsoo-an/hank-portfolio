Hot Coffee

Date of Work: 10th - 17th of September 2024
React, Node.js, Express, Tailwind, Google API, SQLite

As a final project for Dev Academy, a team of four, including myself, developed a service to help users find good coffee shops near them over the course of a week. I primarily focused on front-end work, including database construction and loading page design. This webpage was has been designed with mobile screens in mind. However, a limitation is that all coffee shop locations are hard-coded in the database, so users can only view a few places in Wellington. This is an area for further improvement. I gained experience in the overall process of full-stack development and developed a deep understanding of API design and database management on the back end.
